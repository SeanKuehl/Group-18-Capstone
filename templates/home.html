What is Sonar?

We're glad you asked! Sonar is a social media platform for finding people who share your gaming interests so you can lobby up and play! Sonar is much more than just an alternative to built-in lobbying though. Sonar allows you to find and lobby up with players who are looking to play at a certain competition level, do the same quest or even just play with the same settings and rules. With our platform you can always find the right players to game with regardless of time or region barriers!

We think you should use Sonar if you are looking for awesome games with awesome people. With our platform you can find and play with exactly the kinds of players that you're looking for. No long waits for trolls involved. Sonar also hosts many leagues and communities that offer their own experiences on top of simple lobbying for matches.
